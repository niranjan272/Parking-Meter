Observation:¶

It has been observed that daily revenue for the weekends dates is very low as compared to the reveune generated on the weekdays. and hence it is appropriate to seperate the data into two parts and then build the revenue model on daily basis. Keeping the threshold of 2,500,000 (as per the trends shown above) we will filter the dataset

1. Model Based on Trends¶

As mentioned above, the trends depicts that revenue is weekends are very low as compared to weekdays and hence keeping a threshold of 2,500,000, we will split the data into weekdays and weekends.

We will use SVR for weekdays revenue prediction, while we will find average trend for weekend data
